What we do

The AZSIC Experience

  1. 01

    Finance Education

    We use Thesis-Based Investing, a structured curriculum backed by real-world finance professionals, to cover the principles of equity research, personal finance, and valuation.

    →
  2. 02

    Global Competitions

    AZSIC competes in international investment and business case competitions. This is where members put into action the principles and concepts they've been learning in meetings.

    →
  3. 03

    The Wildcat Tank Competition

    A high school business pitch competition where students present to real-world finance professionals and compete for the top spot.

    →
  4. 04

    Industry Access

    We take pride in our relationships with local businesses and finance professionals. AZSIC holds live Q&As with industry professionals, offering an interactive way to gain insight into different segments of the field. Professionals also serve as judges and guest speakers at AZSIC events.

    →
