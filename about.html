About ASIC

The Next Generation of Finance

The Arizona Student Investment Council is a student-led finance education organization based in Surprise, Arizona. We develop the next generation of finance professionals through equity research, pitch and portfolio management competitions, and mentorship from industry professionals.

Curriculum

Thesis-Based Investing.

ASIC's curriculum is built around our core investment strategy: Thesis-Based Investing. The strategy centers on using fundamental business metrics and macroeconomic analysis to research companies, drawing from the expertise of local investment advisors and finance professionals.

Year-round, members attend lectures and apply Thesis-Based Investing principles through investment simulations and competitions, building a sound technical foundation for real-world investing.

  1. 01 Macroeconomic / Stock Market Index Analysis
  2. 02 Sector Performance
  3. 03 Company Financials and Operations
  4. 04 Valuation
  5. 05 Risk and Portfolio Management

Annual Event

The Wildcat Tank Competition.

ASIC founded and hosts the Wildcat Tank Competition, a business pitch competition centered on promoting entrepreneurial spirit and developing pitch skills among high school students.

The competition challenges competitors of all levels to think and pitch like a startup founder.

Industry Access

Learning From the Pros.

ASIC leverages the expertise of finance professionals to give members direct insight into the processes, roles, and sectors that define the industry. Professionals contribute to ASIC by participating in Q&A sessions, judging competitions, and speaking at events.

The relationship is mutually beneficial. Professionals gain exposure to the next generation of finance talent, while students refine their knowledge through expert advice.
